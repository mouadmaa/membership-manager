## Part 1 – Introduction

**Speech:**

This is **MemberShip** — a membership system for gyms, clubs, or similar businesses.

There are two roles: **admin** manages everything, **member** sees their own data and checks in.

Built with **React**, **Laravel**, and **MySQL**. I will show the admin side first.

---

## Part 2 – Admin Dashboard

**Speech:**

The dashboard shows **total**, **active**, and **expired** members, plus **revenue over six months**.

---

## Part 3 – Plans

**Speech:**

Here we manage **plans** — name, price, duration.

*Add Demo Monthly → Edit price → Delete.*

---

## Part 4 – Members

**Speech:**

Here we manage **members**.

*Create Demo Member → open detail drawer* — subscriptions, payments, and check-ins appear here.

---

## Part 5 – Payments

**Speech:**

A payment **creates or extends a subscription** automatically.

*Select Demo Member → Monthly plan → Save.* Subscription is now **active**.

---

## Part 6 – Admin Check-ins

**Speech:**

Admin can **check in a member** at the desk. Needs an active subscription.

*Select Demo Member → Check in.*

---

## Part 7 – Member Login

**Speech:**

*Logout → login as demo@example.com.*

Member gets a simpler menu: Dashboard and Check in.

---

## Part 8 – Member Dashboard

**Speech:**

Member sees their **subscription**, **payments**, and **check-in history** — read only.

---

## Part 9 – Member Check-in

**Speech:**

Member **checks in with one button**.

*Click Check in.*

---

## Part 10 – Conclusion

**Speech:**

**MemberShip** connects payments, subscriptions, and check-ins for admin and member.

Thank you. Any questions?
